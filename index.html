Welcome to RetroRunner

RetroRunner beta 2.1 is a unified launcher designed to keep your classic and modern systems in one streamlined place. Use the sidebar to pick a system, then configure your emulators and libraries. At the moment I have no idea when Ill let you guys choose the directory of your games, but right now I have to have you guys just use the ROMs folder in Documents of your device.

Each plugins provide a sliver of my childhood to me as a kid, as of 2026 I am 18 years old and I love retro games. My father was a retro gamer and got me into it at a young age. I respect him for that since modern games are trash for the most part dont live up to any expectations anymore.
